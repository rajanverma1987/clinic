## Design Principles for Small Clinic

1. **Simplicity First** - Easy to use, not complex
2. **Quick Actions** - Common tasks easily accessible
3. **Clear Information** - Important info visible at a glance
4. **Minimal Clicks** - Fast workflow
5. **Visual Clarity** - Clean, professional medical design
